Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish perfect for everybody.THis classic Omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection usually combine with ur choice of cheese vegetables and meats.

Preparation time


  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings: Cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats and herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the egg:In a bowl beat the egg with a pintch of salt and pepper,until tgey are well mixed.You can add teaspoon of water or milk for flutter texture
  2. Heat the frying pan:Place a non sticky frying pan over medium heat and add oil or butter
  3. Cook the omeletteOnce the butter is hot and bubbling add the egg.Tilt the pan until u ensure that the egg evenly fill the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional):When tge egg begin to set at the middle but are still stcky running in the middle,Springle you chosen fillings over the Omelette
  5. Fold and serve:As the Omelette continue to cook , gentle raise one edge and fold over the fillings.Let it cook for minutes then serve
  6. Enjoy:Servr hot with additional salt or pepper if needed
